About Our School

ILEX Public School is a premier educational institution affiliated with CBSE New Delhi, offering education from primary to senior secondary level (10+2). With multiple branches across the region, we are committed to providing quality education that fosters intellectual, social, and emotional growth.


Our school is equipped with modern facilities, experienced faculty, and a curriculum that balances academic excellence with co-curricular activities. We believe in creating a nurturing environment where every student can discover and develop their unique talents.


At ILEX Public School, we strive to develop responsible citizens who are prepared to face the challenges of the 21st century with confidence and competence.
