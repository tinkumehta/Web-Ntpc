In Education Service

ILEX Public School is a premier educational institution affiliated with CBSE New Delhi, offering education from primary to senior secondary level (10+2).

With multiple branches across the region, we are committed to providing quality education that fosters intellectual, social, and emotional growth. Our school is equipped with modern facilities, experienced faculty, and a curriculum that balances academic excellence with co-curricular activities.
